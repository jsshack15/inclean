Our goal

Our is to minimize the missmatch of resource and help Clean India initiative by government of india. We want to use the effort of college level students to clean India. For us to operate local people will click photo of dirty place around them. Registered volunteers at our website will be informed: anyone can team up with them. They will go there and clean area and click photo. So, we will be able to see the change.
